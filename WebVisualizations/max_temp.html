Max Temperature


As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as on one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be wamer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
